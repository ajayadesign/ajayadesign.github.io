Hi Chase & Kayla! — The Big Picture

A quick hello — and a vision for your new digital home.

First off — we've been genuinely inspired by what you've built with ENC Printing. It's clear this isn't just a business: it's a reflection of the craftsmanship, creativity, and personal touch you both pour into every product you make, from your signature custom photo magnets to your beautifully detailed 3D printed frames in standard, bohemian, vintage, and rattan styles.

Your current Shopify store has served you well to get started. But as your product line has grown — into lithophane products, frame accessories, downloadable 3D print files, and more — you deserve a site that can keep up with your ambition. A site that works as hard as you do. That's exactly what we want to show you.

What you're looking at is a complete ground-up redesign concept: 13 custom HTML pages, each hand-coded (no cookie-cutter templates), optimized for search engines, tested for accessibility, and designed to look stunning on every device your customers use. It's a custom-built, SEO-optimized experience purpose-built for a family business in eastern North Carolina that takes pride in what they create. We built this to show you what's possible — and we'd love the chance to make it yours.

What We Delivered

  • 13 hand-coded pages — homepage, about, contact, 6 collection pages, 4 product detail pages
  • Image optimization pipeline via custom Python script (WebP conversion, lossless compression)
  • Automated test suite — 79 tests passed, 1 skipped (Playwright + axe-core)
  • JSON-LD structured data on every page for rich search results
  • WCAG 2.0 AA accessibility compliance — zero critical or serious violations
  • Mobile-first responsive design at 375px, 768px, and 1280px breakpoints
  • Directory-based SEO-friendly URLs for every page

Section 06

Mobile-First: Where Your Customers Are

Over half of all web traffic comes from mobile devices — your site is built for that reality.

Designed for Every Screen

Your website was built mobile-first — meaning we designed for the smallest screens first, then progressively enhanced the experience for larger devices. This ensures your customers on phones get a fast, beautiful experience without compromise.

The layout, navigation, typography, and images all adapt fluidly across three carefully tuned breakpoints:

  • 375px Mobile Stacked layouts, touch-friendly tap targets, optimized images
  • 768px Tablet Two-column grids, expanded navigation, balanced spacing
  • 1280px Desktop Full multi-column layouts, glassmorphic nav, hover interactions

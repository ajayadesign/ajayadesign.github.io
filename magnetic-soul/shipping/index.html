Shipping & Returns

Everything you need to know about shipping times, tracking, and our return policy.

Shipping Policy

We want your custom magnets to arrive quickly and safely. Here's what to expect when you place an order.

Production Time

Most orders are produced within 2–5 business days. Custom or large orders may require additional time. You'll receive an email confirmation once your order enters production.

Shipping Time

After production, orders are shipped within 2–5 business days via standard shipping. Expedited options may be available at checkout for select orders.

Tracking

Tracking information is provided with every order. Once your package ships, you'll receive an email with your tracking number so you can follow your delivery in real time.

Free Shipping

Orders over $100 qualify for free standard shipping within the United States. No promo code needed — the discount is applied automatically at checkout.

Shipping Destinations

  • We currently ship to all 50 U.S. states
  • International shipping may be available upon request — contact us for details

Returns & Exchanges

Because every magnet is custom-made to order, our return policy reflects the personalized nature of our products.

Custom-Made Products

We do not accept returns or exchanges for custom-made items due to design preferences, color differences between screens and print, or buyer's remorse. We print your magnets exactly as the design is submitted, so please review your artwork carefully before placing an order.

Damaged or Defective Items

If your order arrives damaged or defective, please contact us within 7 days of delivery. Include photos of the damaged item and packaging, and we'll work with you to resolve the issue — whether that means a replacement or a refund.

Tips to Avoid Issues

  • Upload high-resolution images (300 DPI or higher) for the best print quality
  • Double-check text, spelling, and image placement before submitting
  • Review your order confirmation email for accuracy
  • Reach out to us before ordering if you have questions about sizing or materials

Need Help?

Have questions about shipping or need to report a problem? Visit our FAQs or contact us — we're here to help.

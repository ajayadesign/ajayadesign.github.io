Your original Shopify store presented products in a single-domain storefront. Schema.org Structured Data

Google Understands Every Product

Each of your 6 product pages includes Product JSON-LD schema with name, description, image, price, and availability. Collection pages use ItemList schemas. The FAQ page uses FAQPage schema. Every page has breadcrumb markup. This rich structured data powers Google rich snippets—showing product prices and details directly in search results.

The Evolution

UX Upgrades Implemented

A structured breakdown of every design decision and why it improves the experience.

Layout

Implemented Bento Grid for Scannability

Replaced the standard Shopify product grid with a modern bento grid layout. Featured products like your Custom Photo Fridge Magnets and Glass Pearl Magnets span featured positions, creating visual hierarchy and clear engagement points.

Navigation

Glassmorphic Sticky Nav with Dropdown Shop Menu

Frosted-glass navigation with backdrop-filter:blur(20px) stays fixed on scroll. A “Shop” dropdown consolidates 5 collections into a clean mega menu, keeping the nav to a single line at all viewports.

Design

Premium Dark Theme with Gold Accents

Evolved the Shopify storefront into a premium dark-themed experience (#0a0a0a backgrounds, #d4a76a gold accents) that elevates your handcrafted products. The warm color palette conveys quality and craftsmanship.

Engagement

Scroll-Reveal Micro-interactions

IntersectionObserver-powered fade and slide animations on section entry. Zero-dependency scroll effects that enhance perceived performance and keep customers engaged as they browse your products.

CTA

Moved CTA Above the Fold

Primary “Shop Custom Magnets” and “Explore Collections” calls-to-action placed directly in the hero. Visitors see the value proposition within 2 seconds of landing.

Performance

41% Image Payload Reduction

32 product images optimized with lossless compression, plus WebP versions generated for every image. Modern browsers get smaller files without any loss in visual quality.

SEO Architecture

16-Page SEO Expansion with Clean URLs

Expanded from a Shopify storefront to 16 dedicated pages—each with unique title, meta description, and JSON-LD Schema.org markup. All pages use directory-based clean URLs (/collections/crochet-toys/, /products/save-the-date-magnets/) for SEO-friendly, extensionless paths.

Accessibility

Full WCAG Keyboard & Screen Reader Support

ARIA roles, Escape-key menu/accordion close, focus management, semantic landmarks, and proper heading hierarchy. Zero critical or serious Axe-core violations across all pages.

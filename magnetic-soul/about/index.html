Our Story

It all started with a simple idea: to create something people could truly connect with. What began as a small vision quickly grew into a passion for thoughtful design and meaningful details.

Every piece is carefully made using quality materials, with attention given to each step of the process — from concept to creation. We believe that the things you choose should feel personal.

That's why each piece is designed to be more than just an item — it's something made to be worn, gifted, and cherished, created with intention and made just for you.

What We Offer

Magnetic Soul creates high-quality custom and personalized magnets for businesses, events, and everyday use. Designed to last, made to stand out.

  • Custom Photo Button Magnets & Fridge Magnets
  • Pearl Glass Magnets for elegant home decor
  • Flexible Magnets for versatile applications
  • Handmade Crochet Toys & Amigurumi Plush
  • Magnetic Sheets for crafts, signage, and business displays
  • Personalized gifts for weddings, baby showers, and celebrations

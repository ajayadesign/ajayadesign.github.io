// About

Hey, I'm Riri

I'm a photographer based in Austin, Texas with a passion for capturing speed, energy, and real moments. My lens gravitates toward the automotive world — from late-night car meets and drift events to custom motorcycle builds and rolling shots at 80mph.

But it doesn't stop there. I cover live concerts and events, shooting for crews like Neprican Entertainment and Kamath Entertainment. I love the chaos of stage lights and crowd energy. And when the pace slows down, I shoot portraits and couples — golden hour sessions with a cinematic, editorial feel.

Every frame tells a story. Whether it's tire smoke at CAM's Training Facility, a bassist under purple lights, or a couple kissing at twilight — I'm there to freeze that moment and make it hit different.

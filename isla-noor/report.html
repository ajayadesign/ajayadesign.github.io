🧠 How We Built Your Website to Grow Your Business

This isn't just a pretty website — every single decision was made with one goal: helping your customers find you and buy from you.

1. 🏗️ Schema.org Structured Data — Telling Google Exactly Who You Are

We've embedded invisible code (called "structured data") that tells Google precisely what Isla Noor is — your business name, location, products, FAQs, and contact info. This is what makes you eligible for rich snippets — those enhanced search results with stars, FAQs, and more that grab attention:

  • Organization schema — Tells Google your business name, UK location, Instagram, TikTok, and email
  • WebSite schema — Registers your domain as a recognised website entity
  • FAQPage schema — Your 9 FAQ answers can appear directly in Google results (FAQ rich snippets)
  • ContactPage schema — Helps Google show your contact info when people search "Isla Noor contact"
  • AboutPage schema — Links your brand story to your search listing
  • CollectionPage schema — Helps Google understand your product categories

3. 📱 Mobile-First Design (70% of searches happen on phones)

Google uses "mobile-first indexing" — meaning it judges your website by its mobile version. Your new site is designed mobile-first with:

  • Touch-friendly buttons and navigation (minimum 44px tap targets)
  • Animated slide-out mobile menu with keyboard trap for accessibility
  • Responsive product grids that adapt from 4 columns on desktop to 1 on mobile
  • Optimised images with lazy loading — no wasted bandwidth on mobile data

4. ⚡ Lightning-Fast Loading (Google rewards speed)

Page speed is a direct Google ranking factor. We've optimised everything:

  • Preconnect hints — Browser starts connecting to Shopify CDN & Google Fonts before they're needed
  • Lazy loading images — Product images only load as the customer scrolls down (saves 60%+ bandwidth)
  • Skeleton loaders — Beautiful shimmer placeholders while products load from Shopify API
  • Zero frameworks — Pure HTML/CSS/JS, no React or heavy libraries. Entire site is under 50KB of code
  • CSS custom properties — Single file, no redundancy, minimal paint operations

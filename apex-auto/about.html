Meet the Team

Apex Auto Collision is a family-owned business dedicated to providing top-notch auto body repair services to our customers. As a family-operated establishment, we take pride in delivering high-quality workmanship and exceptional customer service.

With decades of experience in the industry, our team at Apex Auto Collision treats every vehicle as if it were our own, ensuring that each repair, paint job, part order, or sale is handled with care and precision.

Trust us to take care of all your automotive needs, knowing that you are treated like family every step of the way.

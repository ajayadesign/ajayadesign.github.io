Collision Repair

Our collision repair specialists handle everything from minor fender benders to major structural damage. Using computerized measuring systems and factory-approved repair methods, we restore your vehicle to its original specifications. We work on all makes and models, ensuring proper alignment, structural integrity, and cosmetic perfection.

  • ✓ Unibody & frame repair
  • ✓ Structural welding
  • ✓ Panel replacement & alignment
  • ✓ All makes & models
🎨

Auto Painting

Our state-of-the-art spray booth and computerized color-matching technology ensure a flawless paint finish every time. We use premium automotive paints with multi-stage color blending to seamlessly match your car's existing color. From spot repairs to complete repaints, we deliver showroom-quality results.

  • ✓ Computerized color matching
  • ✓ Premium clear coat finish
  • ✓ Spot repair & full repaint
  • ✓ Custom paint options

Hail Damage Repair

Texas hail storms can cause significant damage. Our team specializes in both paintless dent repair (PDR) and conventional hail damage restoration. We handle insurance claims directly and can get your car looking brand new — often without repainting. Don't let hail damage sit — it can lead to rust and decreased resale value.

  • ✓ Paintless dent repair (PDR)
  • ✓ Conventional hail restoration
  • ✓ Insurance direct billing
  • ✓ Free hail damage inspections
🛠

Dent Removal (PDR)

Paintless dent repair is the most cost-effective way to fix minor dents and dings without affecting your vehicle's original factory paint. Using specialized tools and techniques, our PDR experts can remove dents from panels, doors, hoods, and more — saving you time and money compared to traditional body work.

  • ✓ Preserves original paint
  • ✓ Same-day service available
  • ✓ Door dings & parking lot damage
  • ✓ Most affordable repair option
🔩

Frame Straightening

A bent frame compromises your safety and your vehicle's handling. Our precision frame-straightening equipment uses laser measuring to detect even the smallest misalignment. We restore your frame to factory specifications, ensuring proper wheel alignment, even tire wear, and safe driving dynamics.

  • ✓ Laser-guided measurement
  • ✓ Factory specification restoration
  • ✓ Safety-critical repairs
  • ✓ Post-repair alignment check
🛢

Bumper Repair & Replacement

Bumper damage is one of the most common repairs we handle. Whether your bumper needs a minor scuff repair, crack fix, or complete replacement, we've got you covered. We work with OEM and quality aftermarket bumper parts, with expert painting to match your vehicle's factory color perfectly.

  • ✓ Front & rear bumper service
  • ✓ Scuff & scratch repair
  • ✓ OEM & aftermarket parts
  • ✓ Color-matched painting
👁

Auto Glass Replacement

Cracked or shattered windshield? We provide quick auto glass replacement and repair for windshields, side windows, and rear glass. Using OEM-grade glass and proper adhesion methods, we restore your visibility safely. Most replacements can be completed the same day.

  • ✓ Windshield replacement & repair
  • ✓ Side & rear windows
  • ✓ OEM-grade glass
  • ✓ Same-day service
✨

Scratch & Scuff Repair

Minor scratches and scuffs can detract from your vehicle's appearance and even lead to rust if left untreated. Our detail-oriented technicians can repair surface scratches, deep key marks, and paint transfers quickly and affordably. We restore your paint to a flawless, like-new finish.

  • ✓ Surface & deep scratch repair
  • ✓ Key scratch removal
  • ✓ Paint transfer removal
  • ✓ Protective clear coat application

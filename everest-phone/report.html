Why the Old Site Needed a Rebuild

2/10

Old Website

Single page, minimal content

  • ✗Single-page layout with minimal business information
  • ✗No SEO optimization — zero meta tags, no schema, no OG tags
  • ✗No structured data for search engines to understand
  • ✗Generic design with no brand identity or visual hierarchy
  • ✗No dedicated services, about, FAQ, or contact pages
  • ✗Phone grading system not documented for customers
  • ✗Missing mobile-friendly responsive design elements

New Website

Custom design, fully optimized

  • ✓6+ pages covering every business vertical and service

  • ✓Custom brand identity: Teal/Navy palette, Plus Jakarta Sans
  • ✓Complete grading system documentation (8 grades, detailed)
  • ✓Mobile-first responsive with smooth scroll animations
  • ✓Conversion-optimized CTAs, contact form, and map
  • ✓FAQ with accordion, breadcrumbs, sticky nav, social links

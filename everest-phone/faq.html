Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about our phones, grading, repairs, and services.

Grading & Quality

Our grading system ranges from New (factory-sealed, untouched) to Trade-in grade. A+ and A devices look nearly new with minimal cosmetic wear. B+ and B may have light scratches visible only at certain angles. C+ and C grades have more visible wear but are fully functional. Every pre-owned device is thoroughly tested and comes with our 30-day warranty. Visit our grading page for the full breakdown.
Yes. Every single device goes through a comprehensive 30+ point inspection covering display quality, touch responsiveness, camera function, speaker and microphone tests, battery health check, Wi-Fi/Bluetooth/cellular connectivity, button functionality, and more. Only devices that pass all tests are listed for sale.
We specialize in Apple iPhones and Samsung Galaxy devices, covering the latest models and recent generations. Our inventory is updated frequently — if you're looking for a specific model, call us at (781) 325-6897 and we'll check availability or source it for you.

Warranty & Returns

Our 30-day warranty covers functional defects including screen issues, battery problems, connectivity failures, and hardware malfunctions that weren't caused by physical damage after purchase. Simply bring the device back to our Cary location within 30 days and we'll inspect and resolve the issue — repair, replacement, or store credit.
We want you to be happy with your purchase. If the device has a functional issue within 30 days that doesn't match the grade description, bring it back for an exchange or store credit. We inspect every return on-site. Note: cosmetic condition must match the original grade — the return policy doesn't cover new physical damage.

Repairs

Most common repairs — screen replacements, battery swaps, and charging port fixes — are completed within 30 to 60 minutes while you wait. More complex work like water-damage recovery requires diagnostic time and may take 24 to 48 hours. We'll always give you a time estimate upfront.
No appointment needed! Walk-ins are welcome during our business hours. However, if you want to confirm parts availability for your specific model, give us a quick call at (781) 325-6897 beforehand so we can have everything ready.
Third-party repairs may affect your manufacturer warranty depending on the brand and repair type. We always inform you upfront before starting work. If your device is still under manufacturer warranty, we recommend checking with the manufacturer first. Our own repair work comes with a service guarantee.

Carriers & Unlocking

We carry phones compatible with all major US carriers: Verizon, AT&T, T-Mobile, Cricket, Spectrum, and more. We also have factory-unlocked devices that work on any carrier right out of the box.
Absolutely. We offer carrier unlocking services for most iPhone and Samsung models. Bring your device in and we'll determine unlockability on the spot. The process typically takes 15–30 minutes for software-based unlocks. Check our unlocking services page for more details.

Trade-In & Wholesale

It's simple: bring your phone to our store, and we'll assess its condition on the spot. We check the model, storage capacity, cosmetic condition, and functionality. Then we give you a fair quote — you can choose store credit or cash. The whole process takes about 10 minutes.
Yes! We provide competitive wholesale and bulk pricing for retailers, resellers, and businesses. We can accommodate custom orders by grade, model, and quantity. Contact us or visit the store to discuss your wholesale needs — no minimum order required to start.

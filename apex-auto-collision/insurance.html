Know Your Rights in Texas

Many people don't realize they have important rights when it comes to choosing where to repair their vehicle. Here's what you need to know:

  • ✓ You choose the shop. In Texas, you have the legal right to take your vehicle to any licensed body shop. Insurance companies cannot force you to use a specific repair facility.
  • ✓ No steering allowed. Insurance companies may suggest their "preferred" shops, but they cannot deny a claim because you chose a different shop.
  • ✓ You deserve quality parts. You can request OEM parts for your repair. Discuss parts options with us.
  • ✓ You can dispute estimates. If you believe your insurance estimate is too low, we can prepare a supplement to ensure proper repair.
  • ✓ Diminished value claims. In Texas, you may be entitled to compensation for the decreased value of your vehicle after an accident.

What to Do After a Car Accident

A quick guide to help you stay calm and take the right steps after a collision.

Stay Safe

Check for injuries and move to a safe location if possible. Turn on hazard lights and call 911 if anyone is injured.

Document Everything

Take photos of all vehicles, damage, license plates, road conditions, and exchange insurance info with the other driver.

File a Police Report

Always get a police report, even for minor accidents. It's essential for insurance claims and protecting your rights.

Call Apex Auto

Bring your vehicle to us for a free assessment. We'll handle the insurance process and get your car repaired right.

First Time?

What to Expect

Whether it's your first visit or you're a regular devotee, here's everything you need for a wonderful experience.

Dress Code

Modest, respectful attire is requested. Shoulders and knees should be covered when entering the temple. Shoes are removed before entering.

Photography

Photography is welcome on the grounds and outer areas. Please refrain from flash photography inside the main temple during darshan and arti.

Parking

Free parking is available on the temple grounds. During large festivals, additional parking and shuttle services are provided.

Food & Prasad

The snack stand operates on weekends. Prasad is served after Sunday satsang. Only vegetarian food is allowed on the premises.

Children Welcome

Children of all ages are welcome. The open grounds, peacocks, and walking paths make it a wonderful family outing.

Accessibility

The main temple and key areas are wheelchair accessible. Golf cart rides may be available for mobility-impaired visitors during events.

Our founder didn't just dream about unforgettable events — he created one of the most epic proposals and weddings Austin has ever seen.

As a student pilot working on his private pilot license, our founder flew his girlfriend over a field where "MARRY ME" was written in giant letters on the ground. At 1,500 feet in a single-engine Cessna 172, somewhere between the clouds and a racing heartbeat — she said YES!

"If you're going to ask the biggest question of your life, why not do it where no one can run away?" 😄

Arrived by Helicopter. Left by Helicopter.

When it came to the wedding, ordinary just wasn't in the vocabulary. The bride and groom made a grand entrance via helicopter, touching down to the cheers of hundreds of guests. When you love aviation, you make it part of your love story.

"This is why we started Dahal Events — because we believe every celebration should be as extraordinary as the people in it."

Got Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

How much does an event planner cost in Austin, TX?

Event planning costs in Austin vary based on the type and scale of your event. Our packages start from $500 for intimate gatherings and scale up for larger celebrations like weddings. We offer free consultations to provide accurate quotes tailored to your vision. Use our cost estimator for a quick ballpark figure!

How far in advance should I book an event planner?

For weddings, we recommend booking 8-12 months in advance, especially for popular Austin venues. For other events like birthdays and engagements, 2-4 months is ideal. However, we also accommodate last-minute events when possible — don't hesitate to reach out!

What types of events does Dahal Event Planner organize?

We plan and execute all types of events including weddings, engagement parties, birthday celebrations, corporate events, baby showers, anniversaries, graduation parties, holiday parties, quinceañeras, and custom celebrations of any kind. If it involves celebrating, we're in!

Do you provide event planning services outside of Austin?

Yes! While we're proudly based in Austin, TX, we serve the entire Central Texas area including Round Rock, Cedar Park, Georgetown, San Marcos, Dripping Springs, and the greater Austin metropolitan area. We also travel for destination events — just ask!

What if I need to change or cancel my event?

Life happens! We have flexible rescheduling policies and will work with you and your vendors to find the best solution. We'll handle all the coordination so you don't have to stress about the logistics of changes.

Do you handle vendor selection and management?

Absolutely. We have a curated network of Austin's best vendors — caterers, florists, photographers, DJs, venues, and more. We handle all communication, contracts, and coordination. You'll have access to our vetted vendor list with exclusive pricing.

5. Critical Failures of the Old Website

❌ 1. MISSING ABOUT PAGE (404 Error)

The about/about-us links on the old site returned 404 errors. Potential customers looking to learn about the company, meet the team, or build trust before hiring are met with a dead end. For a service business that enters people's homes, trust is everything — and this page was completely broken.

❌ 2. ZERO SEO Foundation

  • No unique meta titles or descriptions on any page
  • No Schema.org structured data (Google can't identify the business type)
  • No Open Graph tags (social sharing appears broken)
  • No canonical URLs (potential duplicate content issues)
  • Gallery images have generic GoDaddy-generated filenames — zero image SEO value

❌ 3. Extremely Thin Content (~150 words)

The entire old website has roughly 150 words of actual content across all pages. Google requires substantial, helpful content to rank pages. Competitors with detailed service descriptions, FAQs, and local content will consistently outrank this site for "estate sales Rancho Cucamonga" and similar queries.

❌ 4. No FAQ Page = No Featured Snippets

FAQ pages with proper Schema markup can appear as rich results in Google Search (expandable Q&As displayed directly in search results). The old site has zero FAQ content, missing a massive SEO and trust-building opportunity for questions like "what is an estate sale" and "how much does junk hauling cost."

❌ 5. No Gallery or Portfolio Page

Estate sales are a visual business — customers want to see previous sales, staging quality, and what kind of items they might find. The old site scattered a few images on the homepage with no dedicated gallery, no category filtering, and no lightbox viewing experience.

❌ 6. No Local SEO Optimization

  • No structured data with business type, geo coordinates, or service areas
  • No service area listings for nearby Inland Empire cities
  • No neighborhood or city keyword targeting
  • Missing from local search signals Google uses for "near me" queries

❌ 7. Poor User Experience

  • No clear CTAs — just a basic contact link
  • No click-to-call phone links (number is plain text)
  • No breadcrumb navigation on inner pages
  • No back-to-top button
  • GoDaddy's heavy JavaScript bundle causes slow load times (~1.2MB)
  • No pricing information visible anywhere
  • No business hours displayed

❌ 8. Accessibility Failures

  • Missing ARIA labels on interactive elements
  • No semantic HTML landmarks
  • Poor heading hierarchy (skipped levels, no clear structure)
  • Missing alt text on images
  • Low contrast text in some sections

6. What the New Site Does Better

✅ 1. Seven Purpose-Built Pages

Each page targets specific search intents:

  • Homepage → "estate sales Rancho Cucamonga", "estate sale company near me"
  • Services → "estate sales", "junk hauling", "downsizing help", "organization services"
  • Gallery → "estate sale photos", "estate sale finds", visual proof of quality
  • About → "estate sale company Inland Empire", brand trust signals
  • FAQ → Long-tail question queries + Google rich results
  • Contact → "estate sale quote", conversion-optimized form
  • Report → This comparison document for the client

✅ 2. Rich Structured Data (JSON-LD)

The new site includes Schema.org markup for:

  • LocalBusiness with NAP (Name, Address, Phone), geo coordinates, payment accepted
  • FAQPage for rich snippet eligibility in Google Search (14 Q&As)
  • Service entries for estate sales, junk hauling, downsizing, and organization

✅ 3. 4,000+ Words of SEO Content

Detailed service descriptions, 14 FAQ answers, company story, service area coverage, and pricing information provide the rich, keyword-heavy content Google needs to rank pages. Compared to the old site's ~150 words, this is a 26x increase in content.

✅ 4. Conversion-Optimized Design

  • Multiple CTAs on every page ("Get a Free Quote", "Call Now", "Contact Us")
  • Click-to-call phone numbers: (909) 519-2353
  • Enhanced contact form with service selection dropdown
  • Trust signals (testimonial cards, upcoming sale banners, payment method badges)
  • Junk hauling pricing grid for transparent pricing

✅ 5. Lightning-Fast Performance

  • ~25KB total (CSS + JS) vs. ~1.2MB GoDaddy bundle — a 98% reduction
  • No framework dependencies — zero JavaScript libraries
  • Native lazy loading on all images
  • Minimal HTTP requests
  • No render-blocking scripts

✅ 6. Full Local SEO Package

  • NAP (Name, Address, Phone) consistency across all pages
  • Geo coordinates in structured data for Google Maps integration
  • Service area targeting for 12 Inland Empire cities: Rancho Cucamonga, Upland, Ontario, Fontana, Claremont, Montclair, San Dimas, La Verne, Rialto, Redlands, Corona, Riverside

✅ 7. Filterable Photo Gallery with Lightbox

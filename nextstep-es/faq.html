Estate Sales

Estate Sale Questions

An estate sale is a professionally managed sale of household items held at the home. It's used when families need to liquidate belongings due to downsizing, relocation, divorce, or the passing of a loved one. Unlike a garage sale, everything in the home is typically for sale — furniture, décor, kitchenware, collectibles, clothing, tools, and more. We handle the entire process from start to finish.

There's no upfront cost to you. We work on a commission basis — a percentage of total sales that we discuss during your free consultation. This model means we're motivated to get the best possible prices for your items. Our commission covers staging, pricing, advertising, staffing, and cleanup.

Typically, we need about 1–2 weeks for preparation (inventory, pricing, staging, and advertising), followed by the 2-day sale event (usually Friday and Saturday). Post-sale cleanup can be arranged within the following week. We'll create a detailed timeline during your consultation.

To make shopping convenient for everyone, we accept cash, credit cards, and Zelle at our estate sales. This helps maximize the number of buyers and the prices items sell for.

After the sale, you have several options for unsold items: we can donate them on your behalf to local charities (and provide donation receipts), arrange junk hauling for remaining items, or you can keep what you'd like. We'll discuss your preferences beforehand.

No — and in fact, we recommend you not be present during the sale. This allows our team to operate efficiently and buyers to shop comfortably. We'll keep you updated with regular reports and a full accounting after the sale concludes.

Junk Hauling

Junk Removal Questions

We handle most household items: furniture, appliances, clothing, electronics, yard waste, boxes, general junk, and more. We cannot haul hazardous materials (chemicals, paint, medical waste) or extremely heavy items like hot tubs or pianos without prior arrangement. Call for specifics!

Pricing depends on the volume and type of items. Small loads start at $150, standard loads around $300, and full cleanouts at $500+. Call us at (909) 519-2353 for a free quote — we'll give you an honest price upfront with no hidden fees.

We prioritize sustainability! Usable items are donated to local charities, thrift stores, and nonprofit organizations. We only send items to the landfill when they truly can't be reused. We believe in giving items a second life whenever possible.

We typically offer same-week scheduling, and we can sometimes accommodate next-day service for urgent needs. Call us to check availability — we'll do our best to work around your schedule.

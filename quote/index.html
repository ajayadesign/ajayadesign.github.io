Market & Event
Photo Ordering System

A custom web application for Magnet Moments Co. — enabling on-site photo collection, order management, and payment at markets & events.

Prepared: March 4, 2026 Valid: 30 days Client: Magnet Moments Co.

Overview

This project delivers a dual-mode web application for Magnet Moments Co. that replaces the current QR-to-Shopify workflow with a streamlined, purpose-built system designed for speed and simplicity at live events.

Market Mode — Buyers scan a QR code, enter their info, select a magnet set, upload photos, choose a payment method, and submit. You receive a complete order dashboard with photos automatically synced to Google Drive.

Event Mode — Guests scan a QR code, snap or upload photos, and images are automatically collected into a dedicated Google Drive folder for the event. No ordering, no payment — just frictionless photo collection.

Two Modes, One System

🛒

Market Mode

For vendor markets & pop-ups

  • ▸ QR code scan → mobile-first order form
  • ▸ Customer info collection (name, email, phone)
  • ▸ Magnet set selection with live pricing
  • ▸ Multi-photo upload with drag & drop
  • ▸ Payment method selection (Venmo, PayPal, Cash, Tap)
  • ▸ Auto sales tax calculation (8.25%)
  • ▸ Venmo & PayPal deep links with total pre-filled
  • ▸ Order confirmation with instructions per method
  • ▸ Photos auto-uploaded to Google Drive by order #
📸

Event Mode

For weddings, parties & events

  • ▸ QR code scan → instant camera or upload
  • ▸ In-app camera capture (snap a photo live)
  • ▸ Upload from gallery (existing photos)
  • ▸ Auto-upload to event-specific Google Drive folder
  • ▸ No login, no ordering — zero friction for guests
  • ▸ Thank you confirmation with event branding
  • ▸ Admin panel to create events & generate QR codes
  • ▸ Bulk photo gallery view per event

Built for speed and reliability with no ongoing server costs.

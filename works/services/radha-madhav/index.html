Who We Are

A Spiritual Sanctuary in the
Heart of Texas Hill Country

Built in 1990, Radha Madhav Dham was created to preserve the devotional environment of the ancient ashrams of Vrindavan. Spread over 200 acres, this ashram is a representation of the holy land of Braj in India where Radha Rani and Shree Krishna appeared 5,000 years ago.

Every activity at Radha Madhav Dham is designed to help spiritual aspirants connect with Radha Krishna through daily devotional sessions, lectures on Hindu philosophy, and celebrations of sacred festivals. All souls, regardless of faith and belief, are welcome.

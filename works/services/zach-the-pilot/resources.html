Essential Pilot Resources

Trusted tools and references every student pilot should bookmark.

FAA.gov

Official FAA resources including regulations (FARs/AIM), airman certification standards, and airworthiness directives.

Visit FAA.gov

SkyVector

Free online aviation charts for flight planning. VFR sectionals, IFR en route charts, and airport information at your fingertips.

Visit SkyVector

1800wxbrief.com

Official FAA weather briefing service. Get pilot weather reports (PIREPs), forecasts, NOTAMs, and file flight plans.

Get Briefed

ForeFlight / Garmin Pilot

Electronic Flight Bag (EFB) apps for iPad. Essential for flight planning, weather, charts, and in-flight navigation.

Learn More

Sporty's / King Schools

Online ground school courses and practice tests. Excellent for FAA Knowledge Test preparation with video lessons and quizzes.

Visit Sporty's

AOPA

Aircraft Owners and Pilots Association — advocacy, training resources, insurance, and the largest pilot community in the world.

Visit AOPA

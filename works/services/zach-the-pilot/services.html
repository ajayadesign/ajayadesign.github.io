Private Pilot License (PPL)

The complete journey from student pilot to FAA-certified Private Pilot. This is where most aspiring pilots begin their aviation career.

  • Minimum 40 hours flight training
  • Comprehensive ground school
  • Solo & cross-country flights
  • Night flying included
  • FAA written & checkride prep
  • Mock checkride included
$65 / hour (instruction)

Instrument Rating (IR)

Fly in clouds, low visibility, and challenging weather conditions. The instrument rating dramatically expands your capabilities as a pilot.

  • 40 hours instrument flight time
  • IFR approaches & procedures
  • Holding patterns & DME arcs
  • IFR cross-country planning
  • Sim & actual IMC training
$70 / hour (instruction)
Start IR Training

About Next Step Estate Sales

Compassionate professionals helping families through life's biggest transitions.

Our Story

More Than Just Sales — We're Here to Help

Next Step Estate Sales was founded by Anita with a simple mission: to help families navigate the often overwhelming process of estate liquidation, downsizing, and junk removal with care, professionalism, and integrity.

Based in Rancho Cucamonga, California, we serve families throughout the Inland Empire. Whether you're dealing with the loss of a loved one, downsizing after retirement, or simply need to declutter, we handle every detail so you can focus on what matters most.

We treat every home as if it were our own, ensuring items are professionally staged, fairly priced, and marketed to the right buyers.

What Drives Us

Our Core Values

💛

Compassion

🤝

Our Journey

How We Got Here

The Beginning

A Passion Becomes a Business

Anita's love for helping families and her eye for antiques and valuables led to the founding of Next Step Estate Sales in the Inland Empire.

Growing

Expanding Services

After dozens of successful estate sales, we expanded into junk hauling, downsizing assistance, and professional home organization to offer complete solutions.

Community Impact

Trusted by Families Across the IE

Word of mouth and glowing reviews have made Next Step Estate Sales one of the most trusted names in estate liquidation across Rancho Cucamonga, Upland, Ontario, and beyond.

Today

Still Going Strong

We continue to serve families with the same hands-on, compassionate approach that started it all — one home, one family, one sale at a time.

Collision Repair

Our collision repair specialists handle everything from minor fender benders to major structural damage. Using computerized measuring systems and factory-approved repair methods, we restore your vehicle to its original specifications. We work on all makes and models, ensuring proper alignment, structural integrity, and cosmetic perfection.

  • ✓ Unibody & frame repair
  • ✓ Structural welding
  • ✓ Panel replacement & alignment
  • ✓ All makes & models
🎨

Auto Paint & Paint Repair

Professional paint matching and application with multi-stage color blending for a flawless finish every time. We use premium automotive paints and clear coats to seamlessly match your car's existing color. From spot repairs to complete repaints, we deliver showroom-quality results.

  • ✓ Computerized color matching
  • ✓ Premium clear coat finish
  • ✓ Spot repair & full repaint
  • ✓ Custom paint options
⛅

Hail Damage Repair

Texas Panhandle hail storms can cause significant damage. Our team specializes in both paintless dent repair (PDR) and conventional hail damage restoration. We handle insurance claims directly and can get your car looking brand new — often without repainting.

  • ✓ Paintless dent repair (PDR)
  • ✓ Conventional hail restoration
  • ✓ Insurance direct billing
  • ✓ Free hail damage inspections
🔍

Alignment for All Classes

Precision alignment services for all classes of vehicles — cars, trucks, SUVs, and commercial vehicles. Proper alignment ensures even tire wear, optimal fuel efficiency, and safe handling. We use the latest laser alignment technology for accurate results.

  • ✓ Laser-guided alignment
  • ✓ Cars, trucks, SUVs & commercial
  • ✓ Post-repair alignment check
  • ✓ Improved fuel efficiency
⚫

Tires & Ag Equipment Tires

Full tire service for passenger vehicles, trucks, and agricultural equipment. We carry a wide selection of premium tire brands and offer mounting, balancing, rotation, and flat repair. Ag equipment tires are our specialty — serving the farming community of the Texas Panhandle.

  • ✓ Passenger & truck tires
  • ✓ Ag equipment tires
  • ✓ Mounting, balancing & rotation
  • ✓ Flat repair & replacement
🛠

Truck Accessories

Outfit your truck with premium accessories installed by our expert team. We carry and install bed liners, tool boxes, running boards, lift kits, grille guards, LED lighting, tonneau covers, and more. Everything you need to make your truck work harder and look better.

  • ✓ Bed liners & tonneau covers
  • ✓ Running boards & nerf bars
  • ✓ Lift kits & leveling kits
  • ✓ Grille guards & bumpers
👁

Windshield Repair

Cracked or chipped windshield? We provide quick windshield repair and replacement for all vehicle types. Using OEM-grade glass and proper adhesion methods, we restore your visibility safely. Most repairs can be completed the same day.

  • ✓ Chip & crack repair
  • ✓ Full windshield replacement
  • ✓ OEM-grade glass
  • ✓ Same-day service available
✨

Detailing

Professional interior and exterior detailing to restore your vehicle's showroom shine. From basic wash and wax to full clay bar treatment, paint correction, and interior deep cleaning. We make your vehicle look and feel brand new.

  • ✓ Exterior wash, clay & wax
  • ✓ Interior deep cleaning
  • ✓ Paint correction & polishing
  • ✓ Full detail packages

Rental Cars Available On Site

Don't worry about being stranded while your vehicle is being repaired. We have rental cars available right here on our lot for maximum convenience. Many insurance policies cover rental costs during collision repairs — we'll help you coordinate.

  • ✓ On-site rental fleet
  • ✓ Insurance coordination
  • ✓ Competitive daily rates
  • ✓ Easy pickup & return

Repair times depend on the severity of the damage:

  • Minor repairs (dent removal, scratch repair): 1-2 days
  • Moderate repairs (panel replacement, bumper repair): 3-5 business days
  • Major repairs (structural/frame damage): 1-3 weeks

Times can vary based on parts availability. We'll give you an accurate timeline during your estimate and keep you updated throughout the process.

Our hail damage repair process is straightforward:

  1. Free inspection: We assess all hail damage on your vehicle
  2. Insurance filing: We work directly with your insurer
  3. Repair method: For minor dents, we use paintless dent repair (PDR) — faster and more affordable. For severe damage, we do conventional body repair with paint
  4. Quality check: Final inspection to ensure every dent is addressed

Most hail repairs are fully covered by comprehensive insurance with just your deductible.

We use computerized color-matching technology that analyzes your vehicle's exact paint code and formulates a precise match. We accept all major forms of payment including cash, checks, credit/debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, Amex, Discover), and insurance direct billing. For insurance repairs, you typically only pay your deductible. We work to make the payment process as easy and flexible as possible.

You always have the right to choose your own body shop! In Texas, insurance companies cannot require you to go to a specific shop. It's your car and your choice. Don't let an insurance company steer you away from the shop you trust most. Choose Apex Auto Collision and know your vehicle is in the best hands.

We offer both OEM and quality aftermarket parts depending on your preference and insurance coverage. OEM parts are identical to what came on your vehicle from the factory. We'll discuss all options with you and recommend the best choice for your situation, repair quality, and budget. We never compromise on safety regardless of the parts used.

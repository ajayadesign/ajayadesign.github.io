Our Story

How a love for shave ice became Manor TX's favorite frozen treat destination

A Family Passion, Served Cold

Sno Ball Dude was born out of a simple idea: bring the joy of authentic, New Orleans-style shave ice to the heart of Texas. As a family-owned and operated business based in Manor, TX, we believe the best treats are made with love — and a whole lot of flavor.

What started as a passion project quickly turned into a community favorite. We saw our neighbors craving something more than ordinary snow cones — they wanted real shave ice: fluffy, finely shaved, and drenched in premium syrups that taste as vibrant as they look.

Today, we proudly serve 60+ handcrafted flavors from our mobile trailer, showing up at local events, festivals, birthday parties, and neighborhood spots across the greater Austin area.

Shave Ice vs. Snow Cones: What's the Difference?

Not all frozen treats are created equal. Here's why shave ice is in a league of its own.

🧊

The Texture

Shave ice is shaved from a block into thin, fluffy ribbons — like fresh snow. Snow cones use crushed ice that's crunchy and coarse. Big difference.

🍯

Flavor Absorption

Because shave ice is so fine, the syrup absorbs evenly through every layer. No more flavorless ice at the bottom — every bite is packed with taste.

⭐

The Experience

Shave ice melts on your tongue like a frozen cloud. It's smooth, creamy, and satisfying in a way that crunchy snow cones simply can't match.

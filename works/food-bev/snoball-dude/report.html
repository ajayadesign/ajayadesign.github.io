Critical Issues with the Old Linktree Page

Let's break down exactly why the old approach is costing the business

🚫 Affiliate Link Overload

The Linktree page has 30+ affiliate links (Hulu, HelloFresh, Savage X Fenty, etc.) and ZERO links to an actual menu. A customer looking for shave ice flavors sees Hulu promos instead. This destroys trust and confuses visitors.

🚫 Invisible to Google

Linktree pages are extremely difficult to rank in Google search. Someone searching "shave ice near Manor TX" will never find this business. Zero organic traffic potential.

🚫 No Menu = No Sales

The #1 thing a food business customer wants is to see the menu. With 60+ flavors available, not showing a single one is leaving money on the table.

🚫 No Booking Funnel

Event catering is a major revenue stream for food trailers. Without a booking form, interested customers have no clear path to become paying clients.

🚫 No Brand Story

"Family owned" appears once in a bio. There's no emotional connection, no story, no values — nothing that makes a customer feel connected to the brand.

🚫 Third-Party Platform Risk

Building a business's web presence on Linktree means zero ownership. If Linktree changes policies, raises prices, or goes down — so does the business's entire online presence.

How the New Website Solves Everything

Every issue above is addressed with purpose-built solutions

✅ 100% Business Content

Every page, every section, every word is focused on Sno Ball Dude's shave ice business. Zero distractions, zero affiliate clutter.

✅ SEO Powerhouse

Schema markup, meta descriptions, Open Graph tags, semantic HTML, keyword optimization. Designed to rank for "shave ice Manor TX" and related searches.

✅ Full Flavor Menu

Dedicated menu page with 60+ flavors organized into 6 categories plus toppings. Customers can browse the full offering before visiting.

✅ Event Booking Engine

Complete booking flow with event types (birthday, corporate, wedding, festival, school), date picker, guest count, and detailed message. Converts visitors into revenue.

✅ Compelling Brand Story

Full About page with the family's journey, values, shave ice education (vs. snow cones), and FAQ. Builds emotional connection and trust.

✅ Full Ownership

Pure HTML/CSS/JS with no platform dependency. Can be hosted anywhere, modified anytime, and will never be subject to a third-party's rules.

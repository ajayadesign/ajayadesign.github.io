Shipping Policy

Last updated: March 14, 2026

Processing Time

Custom photo magnets are made-to-order. Standard orders are processed within 3–5 business days. During peak seasons (holidays, wedding season), processing may take up to 7 business days.

Shipping Methods

We ship via USPS and UPS. Shipping options available at checkout include:

  • Standard Shipping: 5–7 business days after processing
  • Expedited Shipping: 2–3 business days after processing

Shipping Rates

Shipping costs are calculated at checkout based on your location and chosen shipping method. We occasionally offer free shipping promotions.

Event & Bulk Orders

For wedding and event orders, we recommend placing orders at least 3–4 weeks in advance. Bulk orders (50+ magnets) may require additional processing time. Contact us for rush order availability.

Tracking

You will receive a shipping confirmation email with tracking information once your order ships. You can also track your order through your Shopify account.

Damaged or Lost Shipments

If your order arrives damaged or is lost in transit, please contact us within 7 days of the expected delivery date. We will work with the carrier to resolve the issue.

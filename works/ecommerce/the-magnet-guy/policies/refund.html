Refund Policy

Last updated: March 14, 2026

Custom Products

Because all photo magnets are custom-made to order using your personal photos, we generally cannot accept returns or exchanges. Please double-check your photo selections and order details before completing your purchase.

Defective or Damaged Orders

If your magnets arrive damaged, defective, or with printing errors on our end, we will replace them at no additional cost. Please contact us within 7 days of receiving your order with photos of the issue.

Order Cancellation

Orders can be cancelled within 24 hours of placement for a full refund, provided production has not yet started. After 24 hours, cancellations may not be possible as your magnets may already be in production.

Event Orders

Wedding and event orders require a non-refundable deposit. The remaining balance is due before production begins. Cancellations of event orders are handled on a case-by-case basis.

How to Request a Refund

To request a refund or report an issue with your order, please contact us with your order number and a description of the issue. We aim to respond within 1–2 business days.

Refund Processing

Approved refunds are processed within 5–7 business days. Refunds are issued to the original payment method used at checkout through Shopify.

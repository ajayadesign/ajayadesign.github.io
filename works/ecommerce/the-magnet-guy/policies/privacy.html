Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 14, 2026

Information We Collect

When you visit The Magnet Guy website or make a purchase through our Shopify store, we may collect personal information including your name, email address, shipping address, and payment details (processed securely through Shopify).

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Process and fulfill your orders
  • Communicate with you about your orders and inquiries
  • Send you marketing communications (with your consent)
  • Improve our website and services

Data Security

We take appropriate security measures to protect your personal information. All payments are processed through Shopify's secure payment system. We do not store your credit card information on our servers.

Photos You Upload

Photos you submit for custom magnets are used solely for the purpose of fulfilling your order. We do not share, sell, or use your photos for any other purpose. Photos are deleted from our systems after order completion.

Cookies

We use cookies to enhance your browsing experience. You can choose to disable cookies through your browser settings, though this may affect some functionality.

Third-Party Services

Our website uses Shopify for e-commerce. Shopify's privacy policy applies to transactions made through their platform. We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us.

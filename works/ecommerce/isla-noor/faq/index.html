Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our handmade gifts

Home / FAQ

We offer a 1-week turnaround for most orders. Custom orders may take slightly longer depending on complexity. You'll receive a tracking number once your order ships. We use Royal Mail and other trusted UK carriers to ensure your gifts arrive safely.

Most of our products can be personalised with photos, names, or custom messages. Check individual product listings for personalisation options. If you have a special request that isn't listed, please contact us — we love creating bespoke gifts!

After placing your order, you can upload your photos during checkout or send them to us via email at islanooor@outlook.com or through Instagram DM @isla.noor.a. For best results, please use high-resolution images. We'll confirm receipt and send a proof before production.

We accept a wide range of payment methods including Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Apple Pay, Google Pay, Shop Pay, Maestro, Diners Club, Discover, and Union Pay. All payments are securely processed through Shopify.

We are primarily UK-based, but we're happy to discuss international shipping for special orders. Please contact us via our contact page or Instagram DM, and we'll provide a quote for your location.

As our products are personalised and made-to-order, we cannot accept returns unless the item is faulty or damaged during transit. Please contact us within 48 hours of receiving your order if there's an issue and we'll make it right. Your satisfaction is our priority.

We understand sometimes you need a gift in a rush! We offer an urgent order service with an additional fee. Visit our shop and look for the "Urgent Order Fee" product, or contact us directly to discuss your timeline.

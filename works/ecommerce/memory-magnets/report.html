Schema.org Structured Data

Google Understands Every Product

Each of your 10 product pages includes Product JSON-LD schema with name, description, image, price, and availability. Category pages use CollectionPage + ItemList schemas. Every page has BreadcrumbList markup. This rich structured data powers Google rich snippets—showing product prices, ratings, and availability directly in search results.

The Evolution

UX Upgrades Implemented

A structured breakdown of every design decision and why it improves the experience.

Layout

Implemented Bento Grid for Scannability

Replaced the linear Shopify "Release" theme with a modern bento grid. Featured products span two columns, creating visual hierarchy and clear engagement points.

Navigation

Glassmorphic Sticky Nav for Persistent Access

Frosted-glass navigation with backdrop-filter:blur(20px) stays fixed on scroll, replacing the heavy opaque Shopify header. Always-available navigation reduces friction.

Typography

Upgraded Typography for Modern Readability

Dual type system: Playfair Display (headings) + Inter (body) with a 1.25 modular scale. Clear typographic hierarchy that was missing from the original template.

Engagement

Scroll-Reveal Micro-interactions

IntersectionObserver-powered fade and slide animations on section entry. Zero-dependency scroll effects that enhance perceived performance and engagement.

CTA

Moved CTA Above the Fold

Primary "View Kits" and "Start a Magnet Business" calls-to-action placed directly in the hero. Users see the value proposition within 2 seconds of landing.

Performance

78% Image Payload Reduction

11 MB → 2.4 MB. Large PNGs converted to WebP with Pillow. Hero image: 1.8 MB → 97 KB (95% savings). Near-instant visual loading.

SEO Architecture

16-Page SEO Expansion with Clean URLs

Expanded from 1 page to 16 dedicated pages—each with unique title, meta description, and JSON-LD Schema.org markup. All pages use directory-based clean URLs (/magnet-making/, /products/2x2-magnet-kit/) for SEO-friendly, extensionless paths. 10 Product schemas, 3 CollectionPage schemas, BreadcrumbList on every page, and strong internal linking between all pages.

Accessibility

Full WCAG Keyboard & Screen Reader Support

Skip links, ARIA roles, Escape-key menu close, focus management, semantic landmarks. Zero critical or serious Axe-core violations.

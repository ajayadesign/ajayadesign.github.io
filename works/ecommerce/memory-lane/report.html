When a customer Googles you and finds a professional website with a beautiful homepage, About page with your story, FAQ section, and contact form — they immediately trust you more. Mobile-First: Where Your Customers Are

Susie, here's a stat that matters: over 60% of all Google searches happen on phones. When someone is at a birthday party thinking "I should get Mom something special," they pull out their phone and Google it. If your site doesn't look gorgeous on a phone, you lose them in 3 seconds.

That's why we built your site mobile-first — it was designed for phones first, then scaled up for tablets and desktops. Google actually uses your mobile version for ranking since 2023 (called "mobile-first indexing"), so this directly impacts your search rankings.

What we built for mobile

  • Hamburger menu — clean slide-out navigation that doesn't clutter the screen
  • Thumb-friendly buttons — every tap target is at least 44px (Apple's recommended minimum)
  • Smart image grid — product photos stack beautifully on smaller screens
  • Sticky header — navigation follows you as you scroll, always one tap away
  • Full-width CTAs — "Shop on Etsy" and "Contact Us" buttons stretch across the screen for easy tapping
  • FAQ accordion — tap to expand, perfectly sized for thumb navigation
  • Fast loading — images are optimized, code is light, no bloated frameworks
📱 Why this matters for YOU, Susie
Most of your potential customers will find you on their phone — scrolling Facebook, browsing Instagram, or Googling "custom photo magnets." If your site loads fast, looks beautiful, and makes it easy to tap "Shop on Etsy" — that's a sale. If it doesn't? They hit the back button and buy from someone else. Your mobile experience is now flawless.

Your Roadmap to Growth

Susie, this website is your foundation — but the best part is how much room there is to grow. Here's what I'd recommend as your business expands:

🏃‍♀️ Quick Wins (Do This Month)

  • Claim your Google Business Profile — Search "Google Business Profile" and register Memory Lane Studio with your Mission, TX address. This makes you show up on Google Maps when people search "custom magnets near me." It's free and takes 15 minutes.
  • Share your website on Facebook — The Open Graph tags we built will make your link preview look beautiful with your branded image and description.
  • Add your website URL to your Etsy shop — Put it in your shop announcement and profile. This creates a "backlink" that tells Google your sites are connected.

📈 Next Steps (Next 1-3 Months)

  • Pinterest presence — Your products are PERFECT for Pinterest. Visual products like magnets get huge traffic from Pinterest, and each pin links back to your site.
  • Seasonal landing pages — Create pages for "Mother's Day Photo Magnets," "Father's Day Gift Ideas," "Christmas Photo Gifts." Each one targets a new wave of seasonal searchers.
  • Blog posts / Gift guides:
    • "10 Best Photo Gift Ideas for Valentine's Day 2027"
    • "The Perfect Gift for Parents Who Have Everything"
    • "Why Custom Photo Magnets Make Better Gifts Than Photo Books"
    • "How to Choose the Perfect Photos for Custom Magnets"
    • "5 Reasons Every Grandparent Needs Photo Magnets"
    Each blog post is a new page that Google can rank. More pages = more chances to be found.

🚀 Big Picture (3-6+ Months)

  • Customer gallery page — Ask happy customers to share photos of their magnets on their fridge. User-generated content is SEO gold and builds social proof.
  • Event services page — If you ever do magnets for weddings, quinceañeras, baby showers, or parties — a dedicated page could open a whole new revenue stream. "Custom wedding magnets Texas" is a WIDE open keyword.
  • Wholesale / Bulk page — Target businesses, realtors, and event planners who need custom magnets for clients. "Custom promotional magnets" and "branded magnets for business" are high-value keywords.
  • Email newsletter — Collect emails from your website and send seasonal promotions. "Valentine's magnets available now!" to 200 subscribers = instant sales.
  • Instagram integration — Embed your Instagram feed on the homepage to keep content fresh and give Google new content to index.
  • Google Analytics — Track exactly how many people visit, which pages they love, and where they come from. Data = smart decisions.

Our Story

The heart, the passion, and the family behind every magnet we make.

Welcome to Memory Lane Studio 🤍

We create personalized photo magnets designed to preserve your most meaningful memories. Each piece is handcrafted with care and made to last.

Memory Lane Studio was born from a simple idea: that the photos on your phone deserve more than a tiny screen. They deserve to be seen, shared, and celebrated every single day.

Based in Mission, Texas, we're a small, family-run business that pours love into every magnet we create. Whether it's a candid shot of the kids, a wedding portrait, or a goofy selfie with your best friend — we believe every photo tells a story worth preserving.

Thank you for supporting a small business and for trusting us with your precious memories. We can't wait to create something beautiful for you.

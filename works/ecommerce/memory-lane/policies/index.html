Shop Policies

Transparent policies we stand behind.

Shipping Policy

All orders are shipped via USPS from Mission, Texas.

  • Production time: 3-5 business days for custom photo magnets
  • Shipping time: 3-5 business days via USPS
  • Total delivery: 6-10 business days
  • Tracking numbers provided for all orders
  • Currently shipping within the United States

Returns & Exchanges

Due to the personalized, custom nature of our products, all sales are final. We cannot accept returns on custom photo magnets.

However, we stand behind the quality of our work:

  • If your magnets arrive damaged or defective, we will provide a free replacement
  • Contact us within 72 hours of delivery with photos of the issue
  • We'll ship replacement magnets at no extra cost

Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters to us. Here's how we handle your information:

  • We only collect information necessary to fulfill your order (name, email, shipping address, photos)
  • Your photos are used solely for creating your magnets and are not shared with third parties
  • Photos are deleted from our systems within 30 days of order fulfillment
  • We do not sell or rent your personal information
  • Payment processing is handled securely through Etsy's platform

Terms of Service

By placing an order with Memory Lane Studio TX, you agree to the following:

  • You own or have permission to use the photos submitted for printing
  • Photos must not contain illegal, obscene, or copyrighted content you don't have rights to
  • We reserve the right to refuse any order
  • Colors may vary slightly between your screen and the printed magnet due to differences in displays and printing
  • All orders are subject to our shipping and returns policies above

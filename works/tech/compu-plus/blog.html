10 Ways to Speed Up Your Slow Computer (Without Buying a New One)

Is your PC crawling? Before you spend $1000+ on a new machine, try these proven tips. From SSD upgrades to startup optimization, most of these are free and take under 30 minutes.

  • • Upgrade from HDD to SSD (biggest single improvement)
  • • Disable unnecessary startup programs
  • • Add more RAM if you're under 8GB
  • • Clean out dust from fans and vents
  • • Run a full malware scan
  • • Update drivers and Windows/macOS
  • • Clear browser cache and extensions
  • • Replace old thermal paste (advanced)
  • • Uninstall bloatware you never use
  • • Run disk cleanup and defrag (HDD only)

How to Protect Your Computer from Malware & Scams in 2026

Cyber threats are at an all-time high. Here's how to keep your personal and business data safe — without being a tech expert.

  • • Use a reputable antivirus (Windows Defender is actually great now)
  • • Never click links in unexpected emails or texts
  • • Enable two-factor authentication everywhere
  • • Keep your OS and apps updated
  • • Use a password manager (stop reusing passwords!)
  • • Back up your data regularly (3-2-1 rule)
  • • Be cautious of public Wi-Fi — use a VPN

5 Signs Your iPhone Battery Needs Replacement

Your iPhone battery doesn't last forever. Here's how to know when it's time for a replacement vs. just adjusting settings.

  • • Battery drains from 100% to 0% in half a day
  • • Phone shuts off randomly at 20-30%
  • • Battery Health (Settings → Battery) is below 80%
  • • Phone feels abnormally hot during normal use
  • • Significant battery swelling (DANGER — bring it in immediately)

Spilled Water on Your Laptop? Do THIS Immediately (Step-by-Step)

Every second counts with liquid damage. Follow these emergency steps to maximize chances of saving your device:

  1. Power OFF immediately — don't wait for shutdown, hold the power button
  2. Unplug everything — charger, USB, external drives
  3. Flip it upside down in a tent/A-shape to let liquid drain
  4. Do NOT use a hair dryer — heat can cause more damage
  5. Do NOT put it in rice — this is a myth and can make it worse
  6. Bring it to CompuPLUS ASAP — we have professional ultrasonic cleaning

How to Keep Your Gaming PC Running Like New (Annual Maintenance Checklist)

Your gaming rig needs regular love to maintain peak performance. Here's our recommended annual maintenance checklist:

  • • Deep clean all fans, filters, and heatsinks
  • • Replace thermal paste on CPU (and GPU if temps are high)
  • • Check and organize cable management for airflow
  • • Update GPU drivers and BIOS
  • • Test RAM with MemTest86 for errors
  • • Monitor temperatures under load (CPU should stay under 85°C)
  • • Clean up unnecessary files and optimize storage

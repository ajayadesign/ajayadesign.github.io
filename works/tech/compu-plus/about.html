Our Story

More Than Just Repairs

CompuPLUS was founded in Austin, TX with a simple mission: provide honest, affordable, and expert tech repair to our community. We saw too many repair shops overcharging customers, hiding fees, and doing mediocre work.

We decided to do things differently. Every repair starts with a free diagnostic. Every quote is transparent and upfront. Every customer is treated like family. And every repair is backed by a 90-day warranty.

With over 5 years of experience and 250+ five-star Google reviews, our reputation speaks for itself. Words like "fair," "honest," "fast," and "efficient" appear again and again in our reviews — and we work hard every day to earn those words.

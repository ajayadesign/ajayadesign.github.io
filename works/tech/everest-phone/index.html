Quality Assurance

Our Grading System

Every pre-owned device is inspected, tested, and graded so you know exactly what you're getting.

A+

Like New

Fully functional, clean like new. Battery health 85%+ (iPhone). No visible signs of use.

A

Excellent

Fully functional, clean like new. Battery health 75%+. Minimal to no visible wear.

B+

Very Good

Fully functional, very good condition with minor signs of use. Battery health 80%+.

B

Good

Fully functional with minor signs of normal use. Battery health 75%+. Great value.

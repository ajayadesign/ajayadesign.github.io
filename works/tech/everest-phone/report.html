Why the Old Site Needed a Rebuild

2/10

Old Website

3 disjointed pages, no cohesion

  • ✗Splash page — just a logo and two buttons (Wholesale / Retail), zero useful content
  • ✗Grade List — plain text list of grades on a separate page, poor formatting
  • ✗Retail Site — a basic page with broken carousel images and inconsistent design
  • ✗Three separate pages with different designs — no unified brand identity
  • ✗No SEO — zero meta tags, no schema markup, no OG tags on any page

Who We Are

More Than Just a Phone Store

Everest Phones was born from a simple belief: everyone deserves access to quality technology at fair prices. Based in the heart of Cary, North Carolina, we specialize in bringing new and pre-owned smartphones to both retail customers and wholesale buyers across the Triangle and beyond.

We saw a gap in the secondary phone market — a lack of transparency, inconsistent quality, and poor customer service. So we built Everest Phones to be the gold standard: transparent grading, rigorous testing, and honest pricing on every device we sell.

Beyond sales, we provide expert phone repair services, carrier unlocking, prepaid plans, and a full range of accessories. Whether you're a retail customer looking for your next phone, or a business buyer sourcing hundreds of devices, we're here to help.

Quality Assurance

Our Complete Grading System

Every pre-owned device is inspected, tested for full functionality, and assigned a transparent quality grade so you know exactly what you're buying.

NEW

New — Factory Sealed

Brand-new device in original sealed packaging with full manufacturer warranty. Never activated, never used. Includes all original accessories.

A+

Like New

Fully functional, clean like new. Battery health 85%+ (iPhone). No visible signs of use — looks and performs like it just came out of the box. 30-day warranty included.

A

Excellent

Fully functional, clean like new. Battery health 75%+. Minimal to no visible wear — you'd have to look very carefully to notice any signs of prior use. 30-day warranty.

B+

Very Good

Fully functional, very good condition with minor signs of use. Battery health 80%+. May have light surface scratches not visible when screen is on. 30-day warranty.

B

Good

Fully functional with minor signs of normal use. Battery health 75%+. Visible light scratches on body or screen that don't affect functionality. Great value. 30-day warranty.

C+

Fair

Fully functional but with noticeable cosmetic wear — visible scratches, scuffs, or minor dents. Battery health 70%+. Best budget option for everyday use. 30-day warranty.

C

Acceptable

Fully functional with significant cosmetic wear. Heavy scratches, scuffs, or dents. Battery health 60%+. Lowest price tier — functional but shows its history. 30-day warranty on functionality.

Trade-In

We accept phones in any condition for trade-in value toward a new or pre-owned device. Cracked screens, dead batteries, water damage — bring it in and we'll give you an offer.

All pre-owned devices include a 30-day warranty on functionality.

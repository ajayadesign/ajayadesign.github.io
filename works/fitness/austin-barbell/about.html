Our Story

About Austin Barbell

Founded in 2017, we're a purpose-driven Olympic weightlifting club committed to developing athletes of all experience levels.

Est. 2017

Our Mission

Austin Barbell combines world-class coaching, intelligent program design, and a supportive community to help you reach your full potential — both on and off the platform.

We are highly regarded for our excellence and competition performance. Catering to both competitive and recreational weightlifters, our training program offers individual coaching, personalized program design, and a flexible training schedule.

Your Journey

Structured Athletic Development

At Austin Barbell, your development is not left to chance. We guide athletes through a structured and progressive path tailored to their experience and goals.

Every member joins one of our dedicated training teams, based on their current stage in the sport. Each roster is led by one of our expert coaches who provides personalized attention, ongoing support, and targeted programming.

We start by assessing your strengths and movement patterns, identifying areas for improvement, and designing a training plan that adapts with you.
